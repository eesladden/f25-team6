# Project-Name Requirements Testing
## Actors
- Provider
- Customer

### Use Cases
#### 1. Provider: Create Provider Profile, Manage Collection, and Create Listing:
1. Provider Jane Doe signs up and logs in to her account
2. Jane adds a Charizard card to her collection and creates a new Pikachu card.
3. Jane creates a new for-sale listing using the Charizard card.
Jane exits the app.

#### 2. Customer: Create customer profile:
1. Customer C1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.

#### 3. Customer:  View and subscribe to services:
1. Customer C2 logs in for the first time and creates a new profile.
2. C2 views available services S1 and S2.
3. C2 subscribes to S1.

#### 4. Provider:  Reply to Message & Manage Trade Offers:
1. Jane logs in and goes to her messages, responding to any unanswered messages.
2. Jane goes to her trade offers and accepts and completes a trade offer for the Charizard listing.
Jane exits the app.

#### 5. Customer: Write review
1. C2 log in and views their subscriptions.
2. C2 writes a positive review of service S1. C2 exits.

#### 6. Customer: Modify profile
1. C1 logs in and modifies their profile.
2. C1 views service S1 and the positive review.
3. C1 subscribes to S1. C1 exits.

### 7. Provider: Respond to Review & Modify Profile:
1. Jane logs in and reads her reviews and replies with thanks.
2. Jane views her profile and changes her username and background image.
Jane exits the app.
